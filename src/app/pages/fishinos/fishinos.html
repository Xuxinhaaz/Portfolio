Fish Inos

November 2024 - August 2025

The project focuses on identifying and solving technological challenges in the fish farming sector. Conducted by five students and a professor, it aims to develop at least one tech solution while promoting innovation, entrepreneurship, and skill development among the participants. Here

Overview

The project connects embedded devices installed in fish tanks to a cloud-based management platform, enabling the automatic collection and analysis of environmental data such as temperature, pH, and oxygen lev els. Users can access this data remotely through a modern web interface, optimizing productivity and animal welfare.

Highlights

Despite technological advancements in the sector, many fish farmers still lack access to affordable monitoring solutions. The system aims to provide a modular, cost-effective, and scalable alternative that integrates directly with the Fish-INOS mobile application, previously developed at IFPR and available on the Play Store.
