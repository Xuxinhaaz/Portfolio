Fish Inos

November 2024 - August 2025

The project focuses on identifying and solving technological challenges in the fish farming sector. Conducted by five students and a professor, it aims to develop at least one tech solution while promoting innovation, entrepreneurship, and skill development among the participants.

Overview

The project connects embedded devices installed in fish tanks to a cloud-based management platform, enabling the automatic collection and analysis of environmental data such as temperature, pH, and oxygen lev els. Users can access this data remotely through a modern web interface, optimizing productivity and animal welfare.
